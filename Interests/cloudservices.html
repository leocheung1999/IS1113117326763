Cloud Services



Cloud servies is any service which is made available to users which is then provided over the internet. It also can be used to describe professional services that support the selection, deployment and ongoing management of various cloud-based resources.

Cloud services are created to supply easy, scalable access to applications, resources and services, and are completely managed by a cloud services provider.

Cloud services are generally associated with scenarios in which individuals or corporate customers consume the service
